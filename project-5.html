The Five Guardians

AIGC . Storytelling . IP Design . Concept Development

A set of IP dolls inspired by the five Chinese elements—Fire, Wood, Water, Metal, and Earth—designed for a Ningbo-based company. Each doll reflects the symbolism of its element, blending cultural heritage with modern appeal. The appearance, superpowers, and storyline of each IP center around its corresponding element, creating a cohesive and engaging universe.
