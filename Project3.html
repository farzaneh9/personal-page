CalMing

3D Modeling. Rendering . Concept Development

CalMing is a lotus-inspired lamp rooted in the philosophy of Chinese thinker Wang Yangming, who believed that “the mind is the reason,” an internal compass guiding perception and shaping reality. This lamp embodies that sentiment, promoting inner peace through soft lighting and a serene design that encourages mindfulness in everyday life.

Awarded Second Prize at the China Collegiate Design Competition and exhibited at Milan Design Week 2024.

Tools: 3ds Max, Vray, AI, Photoshop
