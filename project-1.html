ThaiTales

UI Design . Graphic Design

ThaiTales is a streamlined travel platform that blends effortless tour booking with authentic storytelling. Users discover curated Thai adventures—from multi-day cultural itineraries to island escapes—then reserve in just a few clicks. After their trip, they return to the community blog to share photos, tips, and personal memories, inspiring the next wave of travelers. Clean, minimalistic design and real user voices create a trusted space where planning meets passion.

Tools: Figma, Photoshop
Challenge
How to create a travel website that feels both practical (easy booking) and personal (real traveler voices) — all while keeping the design minimal, fast, and accessible across devices?
Solution
ThaiTales tackles this with two seamlessly connected sections. Tour Booking presents a clear, curated itinerary—simply listing key destinations with dates —making planning fast and intuitive, with one-tap “Book Now” . Travel Stories is a moderated blog where users upload photos, rate their trips, and share personal experiences, transforming real journeys into trusted recommendations. This creates a simple, inspiring loop: explore → book → return and share.
Design
the design uses a calming Thai-inspired palette (emerald and gold) and generous whitespace to reduce cognitive load. It is designed for multiple devices such as phones, tablets and desktops to make sure it is easily usuable for everyone.
